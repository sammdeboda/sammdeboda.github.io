this week

Anderson Paak

Anderson Paak has been part of the LA alt-rap scene's inner circle for years now, but his big break came in 2015, on Dr. Dre's surprise album, Compton, where he was a major presence and, by some estimates, stole the show. The rapper-singer is most engaging when straddling the line between an elastic chatter and a wrenching moan, remaining unpredictable with a careful mix of fluidity and texture (as on Dre's politically-charged "Animals"). With "Room In Here," the groovy third single from his upcoming album, Malibu, aided by The Game and singer Sonyae Elise, .Paak seems to be approaching a creative peak.
